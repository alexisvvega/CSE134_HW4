Einstein's famous formula: E = m c 2

Despite being in the HTML specification since a long time, developers could not make use of it until recently. Explain why that might be the case:

This might be the case due to the fact that there was inconsistent browser support. Many browsers offered limited or no support for MathML which would lead to inconsistent rendering of math content. For example only until recently does it support Chrome and other chromium browsers but before this Firefox was the only one to fully support MathML.
