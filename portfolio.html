  • Implemented I2C communication and LED feedback for real-time motion alerts.
  • Utilized ITM Console debugging for optimized hardware communication.
  • Developed firmware to manage sensors, radios, and power efficiently.
Role:
Embedded Systems Engineer
Technologies:
STM32, C, Bluetooth Low-Energy
Key Contributions:
Designed motion detection logic, developed firmware, and optimized power consumption.
